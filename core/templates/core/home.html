Job Opportunities Pool

Igembe Elites Platform is a non-profit organization sponsored by Ambassador Kirimi Kaberia who is the Principal Secretary State Department for Industrialization. It is a pool to bring together and link Igembe diverse Professionals to various job opportunities both locally and internationally.

It constitute of Professionals from the three Igembe Constituences i.e Igembe North,Igembe Central and Igembe South Thank you.

Sponsors

Igembe Elites Platform is sponsored and managed by Amb. Kirimi Kaberia who is a career Diplomat having served in many countries. He was the Principal Secretary for State Department for Mining & Petroleum before his posting to the State Department for Industrialization..

"Change in our society begins with us.We are the ones to bring the change we wish it existed." He quoted.

Mission

Our mission is to help humanity thrive by enabling Igembe youth to attain their peaks of productivity and innovation.
